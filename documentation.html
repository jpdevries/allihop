Usage

When to use
When 2 or 3 important links stand on their own
When not to use
When more than 3 links are grouped together (consider a list)
When interacting with forms (consider a button)
When the links do not navigate somewhere (consider a button)
Extra notes
Feel free to use multiple 'bold' variations and multiple 'subtle' variations--you don't need one of each. Choose based on the desired prominence of those links.
The difference between the 'bold' and 'subtle' variations is only visual. To a screen reader user, they are treated the same way. Consider putting the 'bold' links first so they are encountered before the 'subtle' ones.
Screen readers won't treat these calls-to-action any differently from regular links, so make sure their placement makes sense. If you want to put them at the very bottom of a long page, consider putting them at the top as well.
